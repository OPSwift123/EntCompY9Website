Proposed Solutions for Rouse Hill

Based on the data collected and feedback from the community, several practical solutions can help reduce littering in Rouse Hill. These solutions aim to increase awareness, improve waste disposal options, and encourage community participation.

More Public Bins

Increasing the number of public bins in high traffic areas such as parks, bus stops, and the Town Centre would make it easier for people to dispose of waste properly.

Awareness Posters

Awareness posters and signs placed in common littering areas can remind people to dispose of their waste responsibly.

Community Clean Up Events

Community clean up events encourage residents to take pride in their environment and help remove existing litter.

Reporting Hotspots

A simple reporting system for litter hotspots allows residents to notify the council or community groups about areas that need attention.
