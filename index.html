Reducing Littering in Rouse Hill

Littering has become a growing issue in Rouse Hill, affecting public spaces, wildlife, and the overall appearance of our community. This website uses real data, community feedback, and interactive media to explain the impact of littering and present practical solutions that residents can take part in.

By exploring this site, you will learn about the causes of littering in Rouse Hill, how it affects our environment, and what actions can be taken to reduce it. The goal is to raise awareness and encourage positive behaviour that keeps our community clean and safe.

Our Mission

Identify

Collect data from residents and public datasets to understand the true impact of litter in our public spaces.

Educate

Raise awareness about the long-term environmental and social effects of improper waste disposal.

Resolve

Implement community-driven solutions such as clean-up events, more bins, and better reporting tools.
