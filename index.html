What We're Doing About It

Three focused steps guide this project — from understanding the problem on the ground to pushing for tangible action in the community.

01

Identify where littering happens

We collected data from surveys, public datasets, and walked Rouse Hill's parks and streets to map where waste was most concentrated — from the Town Centre car parks to the creek corridors near Rouse Hill Regional Park.

02

Communicate the real impact

Most residents underestimate how serious littering is. We used real statistics and visuals to tell the story clearly — broken down into numbers people can connect with, not abstract environmental language.

03

Give people a way to act

Awareness without action doesn't go anywhere. This site links directly to clean-up events, council reporting tools, and a pledge form — so people can do something concrete the moment they feel motivated to help.
