How You Can Help Today

Every resident can play a role in reducing litter in Rouse Hill. Small actions, when repeated by many people, can make a significant difference in keeping our community clean and enjoyable for everyone.

Simple steps include disposing of rubbish properly, carrying waste until a bin is available, and encouraging friends and family to do the same. Participating in local clean up events or reporting litter hotspots also helps maintain public spaces.
